The Golden Rules

Tricks to save on auto insurance

"ALWAYS SHOP AROUND"

Prices vary from company to company, so it pays to shop around. Get at least three price quotes whenever you need. Online quotes can save you time on this process.

Golden Rule No.1

"COMPARE BEFORE YOU BUY"

Before you buy any car, get insurance quotes to see costs. Car insurance price is related to the car’s price, the cost to repair it, its overall safety and the likelihood of theft.

Golden Rule No.2

"SEEK OUT DISCOUNTS"

Companies offer discounts to drivers who have not had any accidents or moving violations for years. You may also get a discount if you take a defensive driving course or if you are a good student.

FAQ

Frequent Ask Questions
When to buy auto insurance?
You should get quotes or speak with an agent BEFORE purchasing a car, even if you do not have an exact car type in mind. You can compare quotes from multiple providers to get the lowest rate. If you plan to buy a car, fill a simple form now to get your lowest quote.
How to reduce auto insurance costs?
In general, drivers who had experience(s) of at-fault accident pay more than those who‘ve kept accident-free record for years. If you’ve been accident-free for a period of time, try to get a lower rate by filling a simple form.
How to renew your auto insurance?
Most car insurance policies come up for renewal every 6 months, sometimes 12. You can call your agent or do it online to renew your policy. But the RIGHT way to renew your policy is filling a simple form to compare quotes again. 'Renewal' is ALWAYS the best chance to reduce your costs.
